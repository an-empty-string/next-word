What is NextWord?

NextWord's purpose is to analyse how people's minds react immediately after a word or a group of words are shown to them. You can try it yourself if you like; simply go to the main page and follow the instructions. If you're not interested in that, you can view the results that NextWord has collected. You can also send feedback or suggest that words be added to the database using the feedback form.
